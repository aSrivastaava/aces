Cultural Team

ACES’s Cultural team aims to conduct events beyond academics, which will help students spend some quality time with each other, enjoy themselves, and at the same time develop inter & intra-personal skills. As we all know, any team/individual that thrives in different environments must have a clear vision, trust among the members of the group, and most importantly articulate communication. Vision, trust and communication are three very important aspects for any group to develop and this is the aim of our Cultural team. The team will be conducting workshops for enhancing their communication, which will be very beneficial for students to express themselves eloquently and improve their oratory skills. This team will not only help the students to improve their communication skills, but by conducting various events they would also bring out or enrich their skills in non-technical aspects of their career.

Technical Team

Our technical team is here to reduce the grey area between textbook knowledge and real world expectations. This year, our agenda is to establish a regular programming culture, and involve every student of this department to do regular coding which will augment their programming skills. CS concepts must be built up- every single day, and we need a maniacal fixation to improve our CS fundamentals, and our general aptitude and awareness levels. Finance Team

Financial planning is very crucial for any company, business or even for any individual. Thus, no team can function properly if there is no one to handle finances of the group and ACES is no different. Our finance team is in charge of all the money. They are here to take care of the ‘movement of money’ and keep tabs on everything related to the incoming and outgoing transactions. They are also in charge of budget making and ensure that the capital is put to use in an effective way. Their aim is to make sure ACES does not incur any loss, & at the same time raise funds for events as and when required.
